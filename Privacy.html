Overview

CardioScanner ("we", "our", or "the tool") is a free, educational AI-powered cardiovascular risk screening tool developed by Eddala Naveen Kumar, a B.E. student in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at Prathyusha Engineering College, Thiruvallur — as part of an academic project (241ALC401T — Machine Learning).

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use this website at cardioscaner.netlify.app.

The Short Version

The clinical values you enter into the scan form are never stored, never logged, and never shared. They are used only to generate your risk score and are discarded immediately after.

03

Your Health Data

The clinical parameters you enter — including age, chest pain type, heart rate, ST depression, number of blocked vessels, and thalassemia type — are considered sensitive health information. We treat them with the highest level of care.

We do not store your health inputs — ever

When you click "Analyse My Heart Risk", your values are sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our prediction API. The API returns a risk score and immediately discards all inputs. Nothing is written to any database, file, or log.

Specifically, we do not:

  • Store your clinical values in any database
  • Link your health data to your IP address or any identifier
  • Share your health data with any third party, advertiser, or research institution
  • Use your health data to train or update our AI model
  • Sell or monetise your health data in any form
Early Access Email

If you voluntarily submit your email address via the early access form, that email address is stored securely and used only to notify you about future feature launches. It is never shared, sold, or used for marketing beyond CardioScanner communications.

CardioScanner may use the following cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Google Analytics (optional): If enabled, Google Analytics collects anonymous data about page views, session duration, device type, and geographic region. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.
  • Netlify: Our hosting provider, Netlify, may set technical cookies necessary for website delivery and security. These are not used for advertising.
  • No advertising cookies: We do not use Facebook Pixel, ad retargeting, or any third-party advertising cookies. If ads are added in future, this policy will be updated before they go live.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies will not affect the core scan functionality of this tool.

Medical Disclaimer

This is not a medical service

CardioScanner is an educational screening tool only. It does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of a qualified physician or cardiologist with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

Specifically:

  • A Low risk result does not mean you are free of heart disease. It means the parameters entered are consistent with a lower-risk profile based on the training dataset.
  • A High risk result does not mean you will have a heart attack. It means the parameters are consistent with a higher-risk profile and should be discussed with a cardiologist promptly.
  • This tool was trained on the UCI Heart Disease Cleveland Dataset (303 patients). Its accuracy and generalisability may vary across different populations, demographics, and clinical settings.
  • Never delay seeking professional medical advice because of something you read or saw on this website.

If you are experiencing chest pain, shortness of breath, or any symptoms of a cardiac emergency, call emergency services immediately (112 in India, 911 in the US). Do not use this tool in an emergency.
