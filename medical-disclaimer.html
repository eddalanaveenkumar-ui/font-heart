Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: March 20, 2026

🚨 IMPORTANT: This is NOT a medical device or diagnostic tool.
Cardio Scanner is an educational screening tool only. Nothing on this website constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

1. Not a Substitute for Professional Medical Care

The Cardio Scanner tool provides an estimated cardiovascular risk score based on the UCI Heart Disease Dataset and a machine learning model trained on that data. This score is not a clinical diagnosis and should never be used to:

  • Start, stop, or change any medication
  • Ignore symptoms or delay seeking emergency care
  • Replace a physical examination, ECG, stress test, or other medical evaluation
  • Make decisions about surgery, hospitalization, or any medical procedure

2. Emergency Situations

If you are experiencing chest pain, pressure, shortness of breath, dizziness, or any other concerning symptom, call your local emergency number immediately (such as 108 in India or 911 in the US). Do not use this tool in an emergency.

3. Accuracy Limitations

While the underlying model achieves 85%+ accuracy on the Cleveland Heart Disease test set, this does not guarantee accuracy for any individual. The model was trained on a specific population (Cleveland Clinic patients, 1980s) and may not reflect your personal health status. False positives and false negatives are possible. Only a qualified cardiologist can accurately assess your heart health through appropriate testing.

4. No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Using this tool does not create a doctor-patient relationship. The information provided is general educational content and is not personalized medical advice. Always seek the advice of a physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

5. User Responsibility

You are solely responsible for how you interpret and act on the information provided. By using this tool, you acknowledge that you understand it is for educational and informational purposes only. We recommend sharing any result with a qualified healthcare professional for proper interpretation.

6. No Warranty
